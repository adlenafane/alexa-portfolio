An iframe to push the data as its best

The technical constraints give us a part of the solution.
To keep it simple, without having to put our hands in every partners systems, we decided to push data directly to the e-commerce website thanks to an iframe.
It allows to:

  1. Let the Retailer product page header as it is (we were not authorized to access to price and logistical data at this time), and add our content after this part
  2. Make sure that the mandatory information are easy-to-find for the end-consumer
  3. Display marketing and brand content in a fun and easy-to-understand way

Retrospective

Be a constant learner.

This side-project became a killer feature.
It helps the product and the business growing.

We did a great work in a minimum amount of time. The low priority project became an important part of the business. Thinking about what I could do better, I would say spending time to do some guerilla test but moslty thinking to the expected variations and minimize customization: each retailer wanted specific icons, colors and fonts to respect their own e-commerce website guidelines.

Next time, I will test ideas and obviously be more careful with the feature expectations like personnalization to anticipate the come up evolution.
